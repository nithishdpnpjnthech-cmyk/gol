Virtual Desktop Infrastructure (VDI)

Virtual Desktop Infrastructure (VDI) delivers secure, centralized virtual desktops that allow users to access their work environment from any device or location. A well-designed VDI environment enables flexible work models, simplifies IT administration, and ensures consistent user experience while protecting organizational data.
