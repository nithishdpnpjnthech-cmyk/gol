Access Control Systems

GOL Infra Pvt. Ltd. delivers advanced access control solutions that secure your facilities and manage entry permissions with precision. Our services include biometric systems, card readers, door controllers, visitor management, and integrated security platforms designed to protect your premises and streamline access management.

We provide complete access control solutions including:

  • • Biometric access control (fingerprint, facial recognition)
  • • RFID & proximity card readers
  • • Door controllers & electronic locks
  • • Turnstile & barrier gate systems
  • • Visitor management systems
  • • Time & attendance integration
  • • Multi site access management
  • • Mobile access credentials
  • • Intercom & video door phone
  • • Integration with CCTV & alarms
  • • Cloud based access control
  • • Audit trails & reporting
  • • Emergency lockdown systems
  • • AMC, maintenance & support
