AMC and Managed Services

AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) & Managed Services provide structured, ongoing technical care for your IT infrastructure, devices, and systems. These services ensure preventive maintenance, rapid issue resolution, and continuous performance optimization — helping organizations reduce downtime, extend asset life, and maintain operational stability.

A well-managed service model shifts IT maintenance from reactive fixes to proactive management, ensuring consistent reliability and predictable support costs.

What Comes Under AMC & Managed Services

We provide comprehensive maintenance and managed support including:

  • Preventive maintenance scheduling
  • Routine system health checks
  • Hardware & device servicing
  • Software updates & patch management
  • Network & infrastructure monitoring
  • Incident response & troubleshooting
  • Performance optimization
  • Spare coordination & vendor liaison
  • SLA-based service management
  • Remote monitoring & diagnostics
  • Security checks & compliance support
  • Asset lifecycle tracking
  • Documentation & reporting
  • Onsite technical visits
  • Upgrade planning & advisory
