Door Access Control

Door Access Control Solutions are security systems designed to regulate and monitor entry to physical spaces using electronic authentication methods. These systems replace traditional lock and key mechanisms with intelligent access management, helping organizations protect sensitive areas, track movement, and improve overall facility security.

A modern access control setup provides controlled entry, real time monitoring, and centralized management — ensuring only authorized personnel can access designated areas.

Access Control Products

Biometric Access Systems

Biometric access systems use fingerprint, facial recognition, or iris scanning for secure and accurate identity verification.

RFID Card Access Systems

RFID access systems provide controlled entry using proximity cards or key fobs, offering convenience and security for offices and facilities.

Smart Locks

Smart locks enable keyless access using PINs, cards, or mobile credentials. They support remote management and monitoring.

Access Controllers

Access controllers manage and authenticate entry permissions across multiple doors and users within a centralized system.

Door Sensors & Exit Devices

These products include door sensors, exit switches, and alarms to monitor door status and ensure secure access management.
